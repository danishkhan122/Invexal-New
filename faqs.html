General Questions

What is Invexal and what do you do?

Invexal is a premium AI & IoT solutions provider that specializes in developing intelligent automation and smart connected systems for businesses. We help companies transform their operations through cutting-edge artificial intelligence, computer vision, IoT sensors, and intelligent agents.

What industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including manufacturing, healthcare, retail, logistics, agriculture, automotive, and smart cities. Our solutions are adaptable to various sectors and can be customized to meet specific industry requirements.

How long has Invexal been in business?

Invexal has been delivering AI & IoT solutions for over 5 years, with a proven track record of successful implementations across 20+ countries. We've completed 150+ projects and have received 75+ industry recognitions.

Services & Solutions

What AI solutions do you offer?

We offer comprehensive AI solutions including computer vision systems, intelligent agents, machine learning models, natural language processing, predictive analytics, and automated decision-making systems. Our solutions are tailored to specific business needs and use cases.

What IoT services do you provide?

Our IoT services include sensor integration, device connectivity, data collection and analysis, real-time monitoring systems, smart infrastructure solutions, and IoT platform development. We work with various protocols and can integrate with existing systems.

Do you provide custom development?

Yes, we specialize in custom AI & IoT development. Our team works closely with clients to understand their unique requirements and develop bespoke solutions that address specific business challenges and objectives.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience integrating our solutions with existing enterprise systems, databases, APIs, and third-party platforms. Our team ensures seamless integration without disrupting your current operations.

Pricing & Plans

What are your pricing models?

We offer flexible pricing models including monthly subscriptions, annual plans with discounts, project-based pricing, and custom enterprise solutions. Our pricing is transparent and based on the scope of work, number of devices, and level of support required.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial for our Starter and Professional plans. This allows you to test our solutions and see the value before making a commitment. Contact our sales team to set up your trial.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for large deployments and multi-year contracts. Our enterprise plans include significant savings for organizations with extensive AI & IoT requirements.

Implementation Process

How long does implementation take?

Implementation timelines vary based on project complexity. Our Starter plan typically takes 30 days, Professional plan takes 60 days, and Enterprise solutions take 90 days. We provide detailed project timelines during the planning phase.

What is your implementation process?

Our implementation process includes: 1) Discovery and requirements analysis, 2) Solution design and architecture, 3) Development and testing, 4) Deployment and integration, 5) Training and onboarding, 6) Go-live support and monitoring.

Do you provide training for our team?

Yes, comprehensive training is included in all our plans. We provide user training, administrator training, and ongoing support to ensure your team can effectively use and maintain the solutions.

Support & Maintenance

What support options are available?

We offer multiple support tiers: Email support for Starter plans, Priority support for Professional plans, and 24/7 dedicated support for Enterprise plans. All plans include documentation, knowledge base access, and regular updates.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, all our plans include ongoing maintenance, updates, and monitoring. We ensure your systems remain secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally throughout the contract period.

What is your response time for support?

Response times vary by plan: Email support responds within 24 hours, Priority support within 4 hours, and 24/7 dedicated support responds within 1 hour for critical issues.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help. Contact us for personalized answers to your specific questions.
